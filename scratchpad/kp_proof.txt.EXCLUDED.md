# kp_proof.txt — excluded from the public export

The private research log's `scratchpad/kp_proof.txt` was a full-text extraction of
Kang–Pikhurko, "Maximum K_{r+1}-free graphs which are not r-partite", Matematychni
Studii 24(1) (2005) 12–20, used as a working reference during formalization. It is
excluded here for copyright reasons. Retrieve the paper from the publisher:
http://matstud.org.ua/texts/2005/24_1/24_1_012_020.pdf — references to
`scratchpad/kp_proof.txt` in FORMAL.md refer to that document.
